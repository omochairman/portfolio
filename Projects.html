SUPERMART SALES ANALYSIS

JANE AND JINA SUPERMART PROJECT

Project Overview

A Power BI dashboard analyzing sales, profit, customer behavior, and product performance across multiple branches of Jane & Jina Supermart (Jan–March dataset).

FACEBOOK DATASET ANAYSIS

FACEBOOK DATASET ANALYSIS?

Project Overview

This project analyzes Facebook video performance using an interactive dashboard that highlights how audiences engage with content across different days, months, and video types. The dataset includes key metrics such as watch time, stickiness, retention, engagement, reach, and followers gained, offering a clear picture of how well content performs and how audience behavior shifts over time. The dashboard compares KPIs to the previous month to identify performance improvements or declines, helping users quickly pinpoint what is working and what needs adjustment.

The dashboard is designed to help creators, marketers, and digital strategists monitor performance trends on a monthly, daily, and weekly basis. Users can assess which days generate the most followers, how watch-time patterns evolve throughout the month, and how engagement fluctuates by posting day. It further breaks down retention and stickiness across short and semi-long videos, enabling clearer understanding of the types of content that keep viewers watching and returning. KPIs are compared against the previous month to quickly showcase improvement or decline in performance, making it easy to identify successful content strategies and problematic patterns that need optimization.

Key Questions Addressed

  1. What are the monthly trends in watch time, retention, engagement, and followers gained?
  2. Which days of the month and week attract the most followers and highest engagement levels?
  3. How does video type (short vs. semi-long) influence retention and stickiness?
  4. What is driving increases or declines in key KPIs compared to the previous month?
  5. Which performance metrics show strong relationships—such as engagement vs. reach or stickiness vs. followers gained?
  6. How can content creators optimize posting schedules based on audience activity patterns?

Tools: Ms Excel- Ms Power BI- Ms Powerpoint

LAGOS STATE WASTE MANAGEMENT DASHBOARD

LAGOS STATE WASTE MANAGEMENT DASHBOARD

Project Overview

A Power BI dashboard providing a comprehensive analysis of Lagos State waste collection activities from 2020 to 2024, tracking tonnage levels, trip volumes, and operational performance across major waste sites.

This dashboard helps state agencies and stakeholders:

  1. Monitor monthly waste tonnage trends and identify seasonal patterns.
  2. Assess operational efficiency by comparing tonnage vs previous years.
  3. Track transportation activity (trip volume) and understand how movement varies over time.
  4. Compare site performance to identify high-volume and low-volume locations for better resource allocation.
  5. Improve planning and budgeting using historical performance.

Key Insights

  1. Over 9M tons processed in the most recent year (18% ↑ YoY).
  2. 905K trips completed (19% ↑ YoY).
  3. Olusosun is the highest-performing site with 5.2M tons and 514K trips.
  4. Monthly tonnage reveals clear peaks around June and December.

Tools: Ms Excel- Ms Power BI- Ms Powerpoint
